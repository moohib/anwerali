01 — About

Hello World!

Anwer Ali is working as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Art & Design at COMSATS University Islamabad, where he teaches undergraduate design studio and supervises final year design projects. His work sits at the intersection of design, technology and society. He holds degrees in Communication Design and Art Education, and brings over a decade of combined experience in professional design practice and higher education teaching. Before this Anwer has also served as a lecturer in the Department of Visual Communication Design, FADFA, University of Gujrat.

His research spans design pedagogy, generative artificial intelligence (GenAI), visual communication and cultural inquiry, with published work examining critically reflective practice in design education, expressive typography, and the interplay of memory and making.

Committed to problem‑solving through and with design, He approachs his practice as a designer, educator and lifelong learner. Anwer's design expertise centres on brand identity, reflected in a diverse portfolio of client work.
